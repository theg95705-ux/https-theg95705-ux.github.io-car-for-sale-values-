About Vehicle Values

Why we built it, how the numbers are kept honest, and who it's for.

←

Our goal was simple - build a stable, trusted place to check what a vehicle is actually worth.

Values only mean something if people can rely on them. Before anything goes live, we ask around and cross-check with a wide range of traders and collectors - not just one source - so the number you see reflects what the community actually agrees a vehicle is worth, not a guess. That's what keeps this site stable and worth trusting.
